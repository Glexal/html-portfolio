my favorite movies ever

Iron Man (2008)

This movie is absolutely incredible, easily my favorite movie ever. The casting of Robert Downey Jr as Tony Stark is one of the best casting decisions of all time.

Indiana Jones: Raiders of the Lost Ark

This movie is a melting pot of everything action movie fans love, fight scenes, romance, mystery, car chases. Its a classic over the top action movie, what else could a viewer ask for?

Star Wars: A New Hope

Some of my favorite worldbuilding in fiction. I love its unique take on space exploration and how spaceship pilots are similar to car guys who just know their vehicle really well.
